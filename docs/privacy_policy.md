# Privacy Policy — ଖମାରି (Khamari)

**Last updated:** February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Khamari** ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our farm management application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you sign in, we collect:
- Name and email address (via Google Sign-In or email/password)
- Profile photo (if provided via Google account)

### 1.2 Farm Data You Provide
All farm records you create are stored in your account and include:
- Livestock records (cattle, sheep, poultry, ducks, fish)
- Crop, horticulture, plantation, fodder, and agroforestry records
- Allied farming records (mushrooms, beekeeping, organic inputs)
- Daily production logs and financial transactions
- Staff records and work logs
- Activity schedules and notes
- Farm photos uploaded by you

### 1.3 Location Data
The App requests your device's location **only** to fetch local weather information (temperature, humidity, rainfall forecast) using the OpenWeatherMap API. Location data is:
- Accessed only when the weather feature is active
- **Not stored** on our servers
- Used solely to query weather for your farm's coordinates

### 1.4 Device & Usage Information
We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers beyond what Firebase services collect as part of their standard operation (see Section 4).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Account information | Authentication and identifying your farm workspace |
| Farm records | Displaying, managing, and reporting your farm data |
| Financial records | Income/expense tracking and ledger management |
| Staff & activity data | Workforce management and scheduling |
| Location (weather) | Fetching local weather forecasts |
| Uploaded photos | Displaying farm and animal profile images |

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- All farm data is stored in **Google Cloud Firestore**, protected by Firebase Security Rules that restrict access to authenticated users only.
- Farm photos are stored in **Firebase Storage** under your account namespace.
- Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections at all times.
- Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account. Other users cannot access your farm data.

## 5. Data Retention

- Your farm data is retained for as long as your account is active.
- You may delete individual records within the App at any time.
- To request deletion of your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the address below.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Khamari is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
